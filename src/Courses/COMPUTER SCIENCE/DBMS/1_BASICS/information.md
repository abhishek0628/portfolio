🔷 Information

Information = processed, organized data that has meaning

Has context
Useful for decision-making
Derived from data

Example:
“Average age of students is 21.5”
or
“Abhishek is 21 years old from CSE department”

Now it actually tells you something instead of just sitting there looking important.

🔷 Key Differences
Aspect	Data	Information
Meaning	No meaning	Meaningful
Processing	Raw	Processed
Use	Input	Output
Example	101, 102, 103	Student IDs of CSE students
