Core Ideas

Host and Device

Your program runs partly on the CPU (host) and partly on the GPU (device).

Threads, Blocks, Grids

  • Block: A group of threads.
  • Grid: A group of blocks.

Memory Hierarchy

  • Global memory: Slow but large in size.
  • Shared memory: Faster but limited.
  • Registers: Tiny but lightning-fast.

Managing these efficiently is half the battle.
